Egypt

Date: 31 October - 09 November

Status: Upcoming

Best Time to Visit:

October to April for mild temperatures.

Key Destinations:
  • Cairo: Home to the Egyptian Museum and bustling bazaars.
  • Luxor: Explore the Valley of the Kings and Karnak Temple.
  • Aswan: Visit the magnificent Abu Simbel temples.
  • Sharm El Sheikh: Relax on stunning beaches and enjoy water sports.

Greece

Date: 15 November - 25 November

Status: Upcoming

Best Time to Visit:

April to June and September to October for pleasant weather.

Key Destinations:
  • Athens: Visit the Acropolis and the ancient Agora.
  • Santorini: Famous for its breathtaking sunsets and whitewashed buildings.
  • Mykonos: A lively island known for its nightlife and beautiful beaches.
  • Crete: The largest Greek island with a rich history and stunning landscapes.

Spain

Date: 01 December - 10 December

Status: Upcoming

Best Time to Visit:

March to May and September to November for mild weather.

Key Destinations:
  • Barcelona: Famous for its unique architecture, including Gaudí's Sagrada Família.
  • Madrid: The capital city, known for its art museums and vibrant nightlife.
  • Seville: Renowned for its flamenco dancing and historic sites.
  • Valencia: Known for its beautiful beaches and the City of Arts and Sciences.
